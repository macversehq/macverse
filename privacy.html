Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

At MACVERSE, accessible from https://macverse-alpha.vercel.app/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by MACVERSE and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you contact us directly or use our Lead Form / ROI Calculator, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and calculated audits.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Communicate with you to provide strategy reviews and updates.
  • Send emails and process direct integrations with CRM or Google Sheets.

3. Log Files & Tracking Cookies

MACVERSE follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. We use Conversions API (CAPI) to track server-side behaviors to improve ad delivery efficiency in 2026's cookie-less environment.

4. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the right to access, rectification, erasure, and restrict processing of personal data.
